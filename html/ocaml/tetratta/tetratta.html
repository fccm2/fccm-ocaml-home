Tetratta

A board game with a 3D tetrahedral board

Tetratta is a little game similar to chinese checkers, but with a 3D tetrahedral board.

Turn arround the board with the left click, select pieces with the right click, and then the possible destinations are marked with yellow targets, there just click the target you wish to make move the selected piece.
