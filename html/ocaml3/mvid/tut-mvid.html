video-tut

makeing-a-video-tutorial w/ mini-svg or pov-bind.

How to make a music-video, lyrics-video, or any kind of video, with mini-svg:

You can create all the frames, at 24 or 25 fps, and join them all, with ffmpeg, or mencoder.

scripts

Convert the *.png frames with mencoder:

mencoder mf://*.png -mf w=427:h=240:fps=25:type=png -ovc lavc \
    -lavcopts vcodec=mpeg4:mbd=2:trell \
    -audiofile audio.mp3 \
    -oac copy -o output.avi

inkshell.sh:

TMPDIR="/tmp/lyrics.temp"

(for i in `seq 0 2000`; do
   j=`printf "%04d" $i`;
   echo $TMPDIR/ly-$j.svg --export-png=$TMPDIR/ly-$j.png;
 done) | \
 DISPLAY= inkscape --shell

extract-audio.sh:

file="$1"
mencoder "${file}" -of rawaudio -oac mp3lame -ovc copy -o "${file%.*}.mp3"
